It relied entirely on VBA code and SQL. It supported every aspect of our business and often involved writing complex SQL queries from scratch. Hair-raising as it often was, I came to enjoy the challenge of ferreting out documentation, roubleshooting and debugging. Wrong answers pursued lead to right answers. I also developed a real love for working with data, which led me into Python and R.

I recently completed a MERN stack bootcamp and now I want to put those skills to work on a team solving problems, helping and learning, as we build something fantastic.
